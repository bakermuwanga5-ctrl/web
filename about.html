About Us

Faculty of Science and Technology at MRU is a center of academic excellence dedicated to achieving scientific knowledge, innovation and technological development.Tthe faculty provides quality education that blends theory with practical application to prepare graduates who are competitive both locally and globally.

We are home to a wide range of academic programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, Civil Engineering and related fields, all designed to equip students with the skills and competences needed to address real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be a premier Faculty that advances knowledge, innovation and scientific discovery, producing graduates who are globally competitive and locally relevant.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality teaching, research and community service in science and technology; to nurture creativity, critical thinking and professional excellence and to contribute to the social and economic transformation of Uganda and the world.
