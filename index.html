Welcome to Muteesa 1 Royal University Faculty of Science and Technology

The faculty of Science and Technology at Muteesa 1 Royal University is dedicated to producing highly skilled graduates who are capable of addressing modern challenges in the fields of computing with practical skills that respond to the dynamic needs of society. We nurture future leaders, researchers and professionals through a learner-centered approach, hands-on training and community engagement. Our diverse academic programs are designed to equip students with skills that drive development in Uganda and beyond.

At MRU, we believe in building tradition with innovation to inspire creativity, critical thinking and excellence. Join us in shaping a future where science and technology transform lives and create sustainable solutions for generations to come.
